## Typography

### Primary Font: Inter
- **Headlines:** Inter, 900 weight, tight letter-spacing
- **Body Text:** Inter, 400-500 weight, 1.6-1.8 line-height
- **Captions:** Inter, 400 weight, increased letter-spacing

### Secondary Font: JetBrains Mono
- **Technical Elements:** JetBrains Mono, 400-500 weight
- **Metadata:** Small caps, increased letter-spacing
- **Code/Data:** Monospace styling

### Font Sizes (Fluid Typography)
- **Hero Title:** `clamp(3rem, 8vw, 6rem)`
- **Section Title:** `clamp(2rem, 5vw, 3rem)`
- **Large Text:** `clamp(1.25rem, 3vw, 1.5rem)`
- **Body Text:** `clamp(1rem, 2.5vw, 1.125rem)`
- **Small Text:** `clamp(0.875rem, 2vw, 1rem)`

## Usage Examples

### Do's
✅ Use generous whitespace  
✅ Maintain clean, sharp edges  
✅ Keep typography hierarchy clear  
✅ Use gradients sparingly as accents  
✅ Emphasize functionality over decoration  

### Don'ts  
❌ Overcrowd elements  
❌ Use rounded corners excessively  
❌ Mix too many accent colors  
❌ Add unnecessary decorative elements  
❌ Use corporate/SaaS styling clichés
